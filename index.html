Why This Book?

Due to our cultural background, we started our journeys with football soccer at an early age. Later in life, we got involved in Soccer in a more meaningful way, but discovered that there are regions in the world where is not the absence of talent, but the lack of understanding of the game itself that prevents a better performance and enjoyment of it. We hope this book contributes to the enhancement of knowledge for all: players, coaches, fans, and families. Football Soccer is recognized around the globe and brings togetherness among strangers; let us do our part to become better at it!
